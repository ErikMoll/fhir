FHIR Management GroupMaturity Level: N/AStandards Status: Normative

R4B Note: This portion of the standard is considered normative, and was approved as such by ANSI. R4B is a trial-use standard, and won't be submitted to ANSI as a normative standard, so R4B is not considered a normative specification, but HL7 has still treated these portions as normative and no breaking changes have been made in R4B.

This ANSI Normative Standard contains the Observation part of the FHIR specification - the Observation resource and its value sets and code systems.

This standard uses the FHIR Infrastructure standard.
